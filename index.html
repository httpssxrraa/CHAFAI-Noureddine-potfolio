Noureddine Chafai

Senior Civil Engineer

Profile

Noureddine Chafai, a 59-year-old civil engineer with 32 years of experience, is a leader in large-scale infrastructure projects at the Algerian of Highways (ADA). Renowned for managing the East-West Motorway and currently overseeing the Ghazaouet motorway link, he excels in project management, structural design, and team leadership. Based in Oran, Algeria, his commitment to safety, quality, and sustainability drives impactful infrastructure development.
